## Module 36: Gemini Enterprise

## Overview

### From Development to Enterprise Operations

*   **ADK = Development**: The tools you use to write, test, and debug your agent's logic locally.
*   **Gemini Enterprise = Operations**: The cloud platform where you run your finished agents in a secure, scalable, and managed environment.

### What is Gemini Enterprise?

Gemini Enterprise is a fully managed platform that provides:

*   **Managed Hosting:** Serverless deployment for your ADK agents.
*   **Agent Governance:** Centralized control, RBAC, and audit logging.
*   **Data Connectors:** Pre-built, secure connectors to enterprise data sources.
*   **Agent Gallery:** An internal marketplace for sharing and discovering agents.
*   **Pre-built Google Agents:** A library of production-ready agents.
