# Variant B: Standard 2-Day ILT

**Total Estimated Duration:** 710 minutes / 11.83 hours

This 2-day training variant provides a comprehensive learning experience that covers all foundational (Tier 1) and most key skill-building (Tier 2) content. It allows for deeper discussion, more complex labs, and sufficient time for Q&A, ensuring participants leave with a robust and practical understanding of agent development.
